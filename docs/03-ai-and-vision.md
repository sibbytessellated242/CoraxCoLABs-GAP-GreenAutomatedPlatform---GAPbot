The GAP system utilizes advanced Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to interpret and interact with its environment autonomously. We process data locally, eliminating latency and cloud dependency.

## 1. Edge-Native AI (Hailo-8L NPU)

Instead of sending raw video feeds to a cloud server, the GAPbot processes everything on-board using the powerful **Hailo-8L Neural Processing Unit (NPU)** connected via a high-speed PCIe interface.

* **Real-Time Inference:** Capable of running complex models like YOLOv8 at high frame rates (30+ FPS) directly on the edge.
* **Low Latency, Low Power:** Designed specifically for embedded systems, providing desktop-class AI acceleration while maintaining a minimal power footprint suitable for battery operation.

## 2. The Vision Stream (`VisionStream.tsx`)

The processed video feed, complete with bounding boxes and analytical overlays, is streamed directly to the Mission Control dashboard. The `VisionStream` component in our React frontend allows operators to:

* Monitor the bot's point-of-view in real-time.
* View overlaid object detection data (e.g., identifying crop types, obstacles, or pests).
* Inspect confidence scores and spatial coordinates generated by the on-board YOLOv8 models.

## 3. Proprietary AI Modules

While the GAPbot architecture is open, the specific intelligence driving its behavior relies on our proprietary modules:

### 🧠 EcoMind
The "strategic" layer. EcoMind is responsible for long-term mission planning, environmental analysis, and adapting to changes in the ecosystem. It processes historical data to optimize paths and energy usage over time.

### 💡 InnoBrain
The "tactical" layer. InnoBrain handles immediate, real-time decision-making based on the incoming sensory data (Vision, Lidar, Kinematics). It reacts to dynamic obstacles and adjusts the GAPbot's gait on challenging terrain in milliseconds.

## 4. Sensor Fusion

Vision is just one part of the puzzle. The GAPbot combines data from multiple sources:
* **Lidar:** Creates real-time 2D/3D point clouds (`LidarMap.tsx`) for precise distance measurement and mapping.
* **Kinematics:** The 18 servos provide feedback on terrain resistance and incline, feeding into the `DigitalTwin.tsx` visualization.
* **Telemetry:** Continuous monitoring of battery, temperature, and system health (`Telemetry.tsx`).

The integration of these sensors provides a comprehensive, multi-modal understanding of the physical world.
